Tyrion Lannister

Tyrion Lannister is a major character in the first, second, third, fourth and fifth part of the series Game of Thrones. Tyrion Lannister is the youngest son of Lord Tywin Lannister and the younger brother to Cersei and Jaime Lannister. It is important to mention that Tyrion is a dwarf, and he uses hit wit and intellect to overcome the prejudices he faces because of his physical appearance.
